Gateway:
The first ethernet port is treated as WAN port. The other ethernet ports and the wireless interface are bridged together and are treated as LAN port.
AP Client:
The wireless apcli interface is treated as WAN port, and the wireless ap interface and the ethernet ports are LAN port.
Repeater:
The wireless sta and ethernet MAC will passthrough to RootAP, and all network interfaces will be bridged.
